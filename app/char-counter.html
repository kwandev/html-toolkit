UTF-8 stores ASCII as 1 byte, Korean as 3 bytes, most emoji as 4. EUC-KR stores ASCII as 1 byte and Korean/Hanja as 2 bytes, but can't represent emoji and many other characters.

UTF-8은 영문·숫자 1바이트, 한글 3바이트, 대부분의 이모지는 4바이트. EUC-KR은 영문 1바이트, 한글·한자 2바이트지만 이모지 등 다수 문자는 표현하지 못합니다.

SNS character limits

Counts use Unicode code points. X counts CJK characters (Korean / Chinese / Japanese) as 2 and a URL as 23; Premium raises its limit to 25,000. Limits change often — treat these as a guide.
